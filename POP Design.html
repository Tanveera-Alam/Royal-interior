POP Designs That Shape Your Space

Plaster of Paris (POP) is one of the most versatile and elegant materials used in interior design, offering clean lines, seamless finishes, and endless creative possibilities. Whether it's stylish wall panels, artistic niches, layered ceilings, or subtle trims, POP helps shape your space with precision and purpose. It allows us to create custom lighting channels, sleek borders, smooth curves, and modern TV units that enhance both function and aesthetics. POP gives depth to flat surfaces and adds structure to open walls, transforming them into well-defined zones. With its flexibility and clean finish, POP adapts easily to any design style — be it minimal, modern, or classic. Our designs are thoughtfully planned to suit your layout, lighting, and lifestyle, bringing clarity and balance to every room. From entrance walls to ceilings and custom corners, we use POP to create designs that not only look beautiful but also stay durable over time. It’s the silent element that holds your interiors together — shaping your space in a way that feels complete, intentional, and timeless.
